How to Fix "Unauthorized Domain" Error

If you're seeing an "unauthorized domain" error, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Firebase Console
  2. Select your project: verifyai-1053a
  3. Navigate to Authentication > Settings > Authorized domains
  4. Add your domain (e.g., localhost, 127.0.0.1, or your custom domain)
  5. Save changes and refresh this page
